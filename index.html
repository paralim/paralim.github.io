Immortals–William Shakespeare

Hamlet


The Tragedy of Hamlet, Prince of Denmark.

Recently,I’ve read the very first act of the Hamlet,the play astonishs me，with the fountain pen of W.S. I am so honor to enter a world of Demmark, a world which is fulfilled with conspiracy, love, also, paradox.
Which will you choose, love or revenge?

the serpent which kills thy father now wears its crown –apparition of the king

Shakespeare does not only belong to his era but also belongs to the whole world,regardless time and region.His plays like a brilliant sky guide for people to the right direction.What Hamlet does is also the way W.S. thinks.

To be or not to be, that is the question. – Hamlet

Shakespeare in the play, said: “This is a reversal of the age.” Yes, that time the condition of the state of Denmark is weird and dreadful. Wedding followed by a funeral; the enemy arrives at the national boundaries, the palace is still in the orgy; public minister in the court intrigues, and mutual strife; people in the community outrage and some are inciting a rebellion.
Due to the limitation of my acknowledgement on this play. This is what I can provide to you. Now, Hamlet has just known about the murder, and pretend madness, I am so eager to know about what is going to happen next. The plot which is set by W.S. is marvelous. It inspires me a lot,and require a brand new vision to see the world.
